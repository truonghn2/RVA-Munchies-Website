Can Can Brasserie

3120 W Cary St

Can Can is a Richmond staple located on Cary St. in Carytown. It’s a classic French restaurant that’s been around for decades. While sitting down at a table for dinner is always delicious, the pro move is to saddle up at the bar right when they open on Saturday or Sunday morning. I’m in love with their beef tartare which comes with a big basket of warm French bread. Order a mimosa pitcher, kick back with the friendly bartenders and whatever friend you can convince to knock a few back with you at 10:30 in the morning.
