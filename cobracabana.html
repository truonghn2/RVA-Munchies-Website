Cobra Cabana

901 W Marshall St

Located behind the Broad & Belvidere Student Apartments on W Marshall St, Cobra Cabana is a casual and laid-back bar and restaurant. When you walk inside you will find all the decorations on the walls are snake related. They have so many vegan and vegetarian options. My go to is the Chicken Hen Shot First sandwich with a side of Mac n Cheese. I’ve also had the Tofu Hen Shot First and the breading on the tofu is immaculate. This spot has loads of character and cannot disappoint.
