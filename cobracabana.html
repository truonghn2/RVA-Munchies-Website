Cobra Cabana

901 W Marshall St

Located behind the Broad & Belvidere Student Apartments on W Marshall St, Cobra Cabana is a casual and laid-back bar and restaurant with a bit of a punk rock vibe. When you walk inside you will find all the decorations on the walls are snake-related and a giant cobra head hanging behind the bar. They have so many vegan and vegetarian options. My go-to is the Chicken Hen Shot First sandwich with a side of Mac n Cheese. I’ve also had the Tofu Hen Shot First and the breading on the tofu is immaculate. On a recent visit, I made quick friends with two people sitting next to me at the bar. This spot has loads of character and characters, and cannot disappoint.
