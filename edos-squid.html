Edo's Squid

411 N Harrison St

Edo’s Squid is a hidden gem for sure. Located right on campus above Hibachi Box on N Harrison St. Many people easily miss it and the entrance is a little sketchy looking but when you reach the top of those stairs and walk in the door you will be in awe at the bustling Italian restaurant. Every time I have gone, there has been a wait, so I recommend making reservations ahead of time. The portions are huge, and I would say doing more of a family style type of thing than each person individually ordering an entrée. The calamari is on point, and I’ve had the carbonara and mussels in red sauce. Everything I’ve tried is amazing.
