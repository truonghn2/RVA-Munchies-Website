About Us!

RVA Munchies was founded by Heather Truong, a VCU student from Annandale, Virginia who has a passion for food and a love for the community in Richmond since moving here in June 2021. This website is a testament to the excitement and discovery that comes with exploring a new city’s food scene. Join us on this delicious journey through the heart of Virginia.

Heather Truong
